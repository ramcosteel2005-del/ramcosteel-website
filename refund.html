Our Commitment

We want every customer to leave Ramco Steel Trading happy. If something isn't right with your order, we'll work with you to put it right — either through a refund, replacement, or exchange — within the rules set out below and in line with the Consumer Protection Act, 2008.

Return Window

You may request a return within 7 (seven) calendar days of receiving or collecting your goods. After this window, returns are at our sole discretion.

Where a manufacturing defect is involved, the warranty period extended by the manufacturer applies (typically 6 months for our IBR and Corrugated profiles — please check the specific product).

What Can Be Returned

For a standard return, items must be:

  • In their original, unused, and unaltered condition.
  • Free of damage, scratches, bends, or cuts.
  • Accompanied by your original invoice or proof of purchase.

What Can't Be Returned

By the nature of steel trading, some items cannot be returned once sold. These include:

  • Cut-to-length goods — any steel cut, profiled, or fabricated to your specific length or measurement.
  • Custom orders — items manufactured to your specifications (e.g. custom flashings, special colour Chromadek runs, non-standard sizes).
  • Goods that have been installed, modified, painted, drilled, or otherwise altered.
  • Clearance or sale items, unless they are defective.

Defective or Incorrect Goods

If you receive goods that are defective, damaged in transit, or different to what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of receipt. We will arrange one of the following at no cost to you:

  • A full refund, or
  • A replacement of the affected item, or
  • A repair, where appropriate.

Please retain the goods, the original packaging, and any delivery documentation, and provide photographs if possible — this helps us resolve the matter quickly.

How to Start a Return

  1. Email info@ramcosteel.co.za or WhatsApp us on wa.link/q1c2jr with your invoice number, a description of the issue, and photos.
  2. We will confirm whether the return qualifies and issue a Return Authorisation reference.
  3. Return the goods to our yard at Blue Street, Industrial Area, Botshabelo — or arrange a collection (transport for non-defective returns is at your cost).
  4. We inspect the goods on arrival. If approved, we process the refund or replacement.

Refund Processing

7.1. Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method.

7.2. Bank transfers and EFT refunds typically reflect within 7 to 10 business days, depending on your bank.

7.3. A handling and restocking fee of up to 15% may apply to standard returns (not applicable where goods are defective or incorrectly supplied).

7.4. Original delivery fees are non-refundable, except where the return is due to our error or a defect.

Exchanges

If you simply ordered the wrong product or size, we are happy to exchange it within the 7-day window, subject to availability and the conditions above. Any price difference is settled at the time of exchange.
