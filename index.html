About Us

Two Decades of Steel. Built in Free State. Trusted Nationwide.

Founded in 2005 and proudly rooted in Botshabelo, Free State, Ramco Steel Trading has spent over twenty years building a reputation as one of the province's most dependable names in steel and hardware.

What started as a local merchant has grown into a full-service operation — combining wholesale supply, retail counter sales, and our own in-house manufacturing of IBR and Corrugated roof sheeting. Add to that an extensive hardware range, and we've become a true one-stop yard for contractors, builders, retailers, and homeowners alike.

We deal nationwide, delivering quality steel and hardware to every corner of South Africa. But the thing we're most proud of isn't the volume — it's the relationships. We treat every customer the same, whether it's a single sheet for a backyard project or a bulk order for a commercial build. Honest pricing, straight answers, and steel you can trust. That's the Ramco standard.

Products

Local, sustainable, high-quality, and custom steel solutions within your budget.

Ramco Steel Trading supplies all kinds of products to clients — from sturdy 0.22-thickness sheeting for use in informal settlements, to 0.58-thickness sheeting fit for the most demanding structures. As manufacturers of our own IBR and Corrugated profiles, we cut out the middleman and pass the value straight on to you. We serve contractors, hardware retailers, builders, engineers, government, and homeowners across Southern Africa.

We supply an array of a-grade, accredited:

An extensive range of hardware to complement every steel job: screws, nails, bolts, washers, brackets, hinges, locks, paints, tools, fasteners, and consumables. If it's part of your build, chances are we stock it.
Manufactured in-house. Inverted Box Rib — the most popular roof profile in the South African building industry. Square-fluted with a 686 mm effective cover, excellent strength-to-weight ratio, and a broad-flute design for superior drainage. Available in galvanised and Chromadek finishes, lengths up to 14 m.
Manufactured in-house. The traditional S-rib profile, perfect for roofing and side cladding on residential, agricultural, and industrial buildings. Lightweight, easy to install, and one of the most cost-effective roofing solutions on the market.
Zinc-coated steel sheeting offering robust corrosion resistance for both inland and coastal applications. Available in corrugated (S-rib) and IBR profiles, in thicknesses from 0.22 mm to 0.58 mm.
Mild steel square hollow sections from 12 × 12 mm up to 100 × 100 mm. Standard 6 m lengths, with wall thicknesses of 1.2 mm, 1.5 mm, 1.9 mm and heavier on request. Ideal for gates, frames, and structural work.
Mild steel and galvanised round tubing in a wide range of diameters. Standard 6 m lengths. Used widely in handrails, frames, fencing, and general fabrication.
Mild steel rectangular hollow sections in popular sizes from 38 × 25 mm up to 100 × 50 mm and larger on request. Standard 6 m lengths. The go-to section for gates, trailers, awnings, and structural framing.
The classic South African security fencing. Devil fork panels with pointed spear tops, supplied with matching posts and ready for installation. Strong, low-maintenance, and an effective deterrent for homes, businesses, schools, and industrial sites. Various panel heights available.
Pre-painted galvanised steel sheeting available in a full range of colours, letting clients personalise their projects efficiently. Up to 80% lighter than concrete roofing and engineered for South Africa's harsh climate.
