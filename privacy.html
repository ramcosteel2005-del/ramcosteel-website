Introduction

Ramco Steel Trading CC ("Ramco Steel", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to our website at ramcosteel.co.za and to any interaction you have with us by phone, email, WhatsApp, or in person at our yard.

This policy is drafted in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you give us, or that we need to provide our services. This may include:

  • Contact details — name, surname, phone number, email address, and physical or delivery address.
  • Quote and order information — the products you ask about, quantities, project details, drawings or documents you upload.
  • Payment details — handled by our bank or payment processor; we do not store full card numbers on our website.
  • Website usage data — pages visited, device type, browser, and approximate location, collected automatically by basic analytics.

How We Use It

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to quotes, enquiries, and orders.
  • Process payments and arrange delivery.
  • Keep records for accounting, tax, and legal compliance.
  • Send you transactional messages about your order or quote (we do not send marketing without your consent).
  • Improve our website, products, and customer service.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with third parties who help us run the business, and only as far as needed to do their job. These may include:

  • Couriers and transport partners (for delivery).
  • Our bank and payment processors.
  • Our accountants, auditors, and legal advisors.
  • Government and regulatory bodies, where required by law.

Where data leaves South Africa, we ensure the recipient offers an adequate level of protection.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to keep the site working properly and to understand how visitors use it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings — the site will still work, but some convenience features may not.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete inaccurate information.
  • Object to or withdraw consent for certain processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa (inforegulator.org.za) if you believe your rights have been infringed.

To exercise these rights, email info@ramcosteel.co.za.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as it is needed to provide our services, comply with our legal obligations (such as SARS record-keeping requirements), resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Children

Our services are not intended for persons under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any change. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
