Overview

Ramco Steel Trading offers nationwide delivery across all nine provinces of South Africa, as well as collection from our yard in Botshabelo. This policy explains how we handle delivery, the costs involved, and what to expect.

Where We Deliver

We deliver throughout South Africa, including:

  • Free State, Gauteng, Eastern Cape, Northern Cape, North West, KwaZulu-Natal, Western Cape, Mpumalanga, and Limpopo.
  • Cross-border deliveries into neighbouring SADC countries (Lesotho, Botswana, Eswatini, Namibia, Zimbabwe, Mozambique) are arranged on request — please contact us for a custom quote.

Delivery Options

Depending on order size, destination, and product type, we use:

  • Our own fleet for Free State and nearby provinces — direct, reliable, and trackable.
  • Trusted long-haul transport partners for cross-country deliveries and larger consignments.
  • Specialist transport for over-length roof sheeting (sheets above 6 m) and heavy bulk loads.

Delivery Fees

Delivery is quoted per order based on:

  • The destination (distance from Botshabelo).
  • The weight, length, and volume of the goods.
  • The level of off-loading equipment required at delivery (crane truck, forklift, manual).
  • The vehicle access on site.

Your delivery quote is confirmed in writing before you accept the order — no hidden fees.

Lead Times

Typical lead times once payment has cleared (or credit terms confirmed):

  • In-stock items, Free State & nearby: 1–3 business days.
  • In-stock items, other provinces: 3–7 business days.
  • Manufactured-to-order items (custom-length IBR or Corrugated, Chromadek colour orders): 3–10 business days, depending on volume and current production schedule.

These are good-faith estimates, not guarantees. Final delivery date is confirmed with you at order acknowledgement.

Collection From Our Yard

You're welcome to collect your order from our yard in Botshabelo. Please:

  • Wait for our written confirmation that the order is ready before arriving.
  • Bring your invoice number and the name of the person who placed the order.
  • Bring a suitable vehicle. Long sheets (over 3 m) require an appropriate trailer or bakkie with secure overhang — we cannot load goods onto a vehicle that is unsafe to transport them.

Off-loading & Site Access

7.1. Unless agreed otherwise in writing, you are responsible for off-loading goods on arrival and for providing safe access for the delivery vehicle.

7.2. If our driver arrives and the site is inaccessible, or there is no one available to assist or accept the delivery, a failed-delivery fee may apply for the return trip.

7.3. Where off-loading is included in your delivery quote, the driver will off-load to the nearest safe point at the address — not necessarily inside a building or up flights of stairs.

Risk & Damage

8.1. Risk in the goods passes to you on delivery (or collection).

8.2. You must inspect the goods at the point of delivery and note any visible damage on the delivery note before signing. We cannot honour transit damage claims that were not recorded at delivery.

8.3. Hidden defects discovered after delivery must be reported within 48 hours — see our Refund & Return Policy.

Delays

While we work hard to deliver on time, delays can occur due to weather, vehicle breakdowns, supplier shortages, load-shedding, road closures, or other factors outside our control. We will keep you informed and reschedule as quickly as possible, but we are not liable for indirect losses (such as missed project deadlines) caused by such delays.
